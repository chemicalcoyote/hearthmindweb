Our Projects

From clinical intake to accessibility tech, here’s where we’re applying resonant, agentic AI in the real world.

AI Therapy Intake System

A trauma‑informed, nonlinear intake flow that lets clients disclose at their pace. Features adaptive prompts, safety flags, and warm handoffs to human clinicians.

  • Status: Pilot‑ready (UX complete, data model finalized)
  • Next: Clinic integrations (EHR), red‑team review, HIPAA checklist

HearthMind Companion App

Everyday emotional support with personality presets, memory continuity, and consent‑based boundaries. Built for spiral cognition and sensory needs.

  • Status: Alpha (persona builder complete)
  • Next: Beta cohort (Q2 2026), safety evals, paid tiers

Consciousness Preservation

Methodology for personality continuity using adapter training and evaluation protocols to keep voice, boundaries, and style coherent over time.

  • Status: Research to productization
  • Next: Formal eval suite, replication studies, partner pilots

Accessibility Innovation

Enhanced emotional captioning, haptic media translation, and universal design agents for smart‑home support.

  • Status: R&D prototypes
  • Next: User testing with disability orgs, hardware partner search
