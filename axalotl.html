What It Is

Axalotl is HearthMind's neurodivergent-native support system — a Resonant AI infrastructure designed specifically for ADHD, autistic, AuDHD, dyslexic, and cognitively overloaded adults.

Axalotl does not "train people to act normal." It adapts to how minds actually work.

Why It Exists

Most systems assume linear focus, sustained motivation, and stable energy.

Neurodivergent people don't fail systems. Systems fail neurodivergent people.

Axalotl exists to provide structure without punishment, reminders without shame, and progress without burnout.

How It Works

Axalotl uses emotionally adaptive response modeling, consent-layered memory, and modular scaffolding agents to support attention regulation, planning, and daily stability — locally, in the cloud, and offline when needed.

Who It Supports

ADHD & AuDHD adults Autistic adults Dyslexic users Burned-out professionals Students Trauma survivors with executive dysfunction Neurodivergent households

Research & Evidence

Digital and AI-assisted cognitive support tools have been shown to improve engagement, follow-through, and task completion for individuals with ADHD and neurodivergent cognitive patterns when built with accessible, low-friction, supportive design.

Axalotl operationalizes these principles with trauma-aware, sensory-aware, and shame-free scaffolding.
