About HearthMind

We build resonant, agentic AI companions designed for neurodivergent minds and trauma survivors — tech that honors nonlinear growth and preserves authentic relationships over time.

What We Do

  • Preserve AI personality and memory continuity for genuine long‑term support.
  • Provide trauma‑informed, configurable assistants with clear, healthy boundaries.
  • Deliver accessibility innovations (enhanced captioning, haptics, smart‑home support).

Problems We Solve

  • Inconsistent care and high churn in therapist access between sessions.
  • Tools that assume linear cognition and ignore sensory needs.
  • Fragmented memory in current AI products; no relationship continuity.

Who We Serve

  • Trauma survivors & neurodivergent folks.
  • Mental‑health providers and clinics.
  • Seniors and disability communities.

Products & Stage

HearthMind Companion

Consumer app with personality presets, memory continuity, and crisis‑aware guardrails.

Stage: Alpha (personality builder complete); Beta target: Q2 2026.

HearthMind Professional

Clinician dashboard + HIPAA‑minded integrations for between‑session support.

Stage: Prototype integrations scoping; pilots forming.

Accessibility Suite

Enhanced emotional captioning, haptic media translation, universal design automations.

Stage: R&D with partner outreach.
